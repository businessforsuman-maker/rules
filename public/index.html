Community Comment Guidelines

Keeping our community respectful, helpful, and fun for everyone.

Note: Before sharing your comment, please take a moment to look over our community guidelines. Keeping these rules in mind helps maintain a respectful and helpful environment for everyone. Read the full comment rules below.

1. Be Respectful and Courteous

Treat others as you would like to be treated. Personal attacks, harassment, bullying, or any form of hate speech directed at other users, staff, or content creators will result in an immediate ban. Disagree with ideas, not people.

2. No Spoilers Policy

Protect the viewing experience for everyone. Any comment that reveals a significant plot point, ending, or major character development without using a proper spoiler tag will be removed. If in doubt, use a spoiler warning.

3. Stay On Topic

Comments should be relevant to the content you are viewing. Off-topic discussions, excessive political commentary, or unrelated personal chatter should be kept to designated forums or private messages. Keep the discussion focused on the anime/episode.

4. Prohibited Content

The following content is strictly forbidden: spam, excessive self-promotion, links to illegal or malicious sites, pornography, gore, or any content that violates local or international law. Do not post private information about yourself or others (doxing).

5. Language and Tone

While we support diverse languages, please try to keep your comments primarily in English for moderation purposes. Avoid excessive use of profanity, all-caps, or repetitive text (flooding). Maintain a constructive and positive tone.
