Official Community Comment Guidelines

Detailed rules that apply to all comments, replies, and user interactions on 9Anime.

Important: Every user must follow these rules. Violations will result in comment deletion, warnings, mutes, or permanent bans depending on severity.

1. Be Respectful & Civil

Personal attacks, insults, harassment, bullying, or hate speech are strictly forbidden. This includes targeting someone’s:

  • Race, religion, gender, sexuality, nationality, appearance, disability, or age
  • Repeatedly targeting or stalking another user
  • Threats of any kind (including doxxing or real-life harm)

Disagree with ideas — never attack people.

2. No Spoilers Without Proper Tags

Any information about future episodes, manga chapters, light novels, endings, character deaths, or major plot twists MUST be hidden.

  • Use the spoiler button or type: [spoiler]text here[/spoiler]
  • Even small hints count as spoilers
  • Spoilers in usernames or profile pictures are not allowed
  • First offense = deletion + warning | Repeated = mute/ban

3. Stay On-Topic

Comments must be related to the anime or episode you’re watching.

  • No political or religious debates
  • No drama or fights about other streaming sites
  • No begging for links, downloads, or release dates
  • No “Episode X when?” spam

4. No Illegal or Harmful Content

Instant permanent ban (no warning) for posting:

  • Links to torrents, downloads, or other streaming sites
  • Pornography, gore, shock images, or NSFW content
  • Malware, phishing links, or scams
  • Anyone’s personal information (doxxing)
  • Threats or encouragement of violence/self-harm

5. No Spam or Self-Promotion

Automatically removed:

  • Repetitive messages, copypastas, or flooding
  • Discord invites, YouTube/TikTok channels, referral links
  • Begging for likes, subscribers, or followers
  • Advertising any product or service

6. Language & Behavior

  • English is required for moderation — keep most of your comment in English
  • Short phrases in other languages are fine
  • Excessive swearing, ALL CAPS rage, or toxic behavior will be removed
  • Impersonating moderators or staff = permanent ban

7. Enforcement & Punishments

Minor violations (first-time spoilers, mild toxicity):

  • 1st → Warning
  • 2nd → 24–72 hour mute
  • 3rd → 7–30 day ban

Serious violations (hate speech, illegal links, doxxing, threats) → Immediate permanent ban, no appeal.
