Keeping the Vibe Right: Our Simple Community Guidelines

These are the common-sense rules that apply to every single comment, reply, and interaction you have here on 9Anime. Let's make this a great place for everyone!

Heads Up: Every single person needs to follow these. If you break them, we might have to delete your comment, send a warning, mute you for a bit, or, for the really serious stuff, give you the permanent boot. We're just trying to keep the peace!

1. Be a Decent Human: Respect & Civility

We're all here because we love anime, so let's keep the conversation fun and friendly. Absolutely no personal attacks, insults, bullying, or any kind of hate speech. This is a zero-tolerance zone for:

  • Targeting someone based on who they are (race, gender, religion, etc.)
  • Repeatedly harassing or stalking another user—that's just creepy.
  • Threats of any kind (including digging up and sharing personal info, or threats of real-life harm).

It's totally fine to disagree with an idea or a show's plot, but **never** attack the person behind the keyboard.

2. Seriously, Tag Your Spoilers!

Nobody likes having a major plot twist ruined! If you're talking about anything that hasn't happened in the current episode—whether it's from the manga, light novel, or even a future episode—you **must** hide it.

  • Use the spoiler button or type it out like this: [spoiler]text here[/spoiler]
  • Even tiny hints count as spoilers, so when in doubt, tag it!
  • Don't put spoilers in your username or profile picture—that's just mean.
  • First time you forget? We'll delete it and send a warning. Keep doing it? You'll get muted or banned.

3. Keep It About the Anime

Let's keep the comments focused on the show we're all watching. We're not the place for:

  • Political or religious debates—take those elsewhere, please.
  • Starting drama or fights about other streaming sites.
  • Begging for links, downloads, or asking when the next episode drops (we'll post it when it's ready!).
  • Spamming "Episode X when?"—it just clogs up the feed.

4. No Shady or Harmful Stuff (Instant Ban)

This is the serious stuff. If you post any of the following, you're getting an **immediate, permanent ban** with no warning. We have to protect the community:

  • Links to torrents, illegal downloads, or other streaming sites.
  • Pornography, gore, shock images, or anything NSFW.
  • Malware, phishing links, or scams—don't try it.
  • Sharing anyone's personal information (doxxing).
  • Threats or encouraging violence/self-harm.

5. No Spamming or Shameless Self-Promotion

Our filters are pretty good at catching this, and it will be automatically removed:

  • Repetitive messages, copypastas, or flooding the comments.
  • Posting Discord invites, YouTube/TikTok channels, or referral links.
  • Begging for likes, subscribers, or followers—earn them!
  • Advertising any product or service.

6. Language & How You Act

  • We need to be able to moderate, so please keep the majority of your comment in **English**.
  • Short phrases in other languages are totally fine!
  • Excessive swearing, screaming in ALL CAPS, or generally toxic behavior will be removed.
  • Don't try to pretend you're a moderator or staff member—that's an instant permanent ban.

7. How We Enforce Things (The Mod Process)

We try to be fair and consistent. For minor slip-ups (like forgetting a spoiler tag or being a little too spicy):

  • **First Time:** A friendly warning.
  • **Second Time:** A temporary mute (24–72 hours).
  • **Third Time:** A longer temporary ban (7–30 days).

For the really bad stuff (hate speech, illegal links, threats, doxxing) → **Immediate permanent ban, no exceptions, no appeal.** We take the safety of our community seriously.
