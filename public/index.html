9anime — Comment Rules

A short, clear policy for comments and community behaviour
Core Rules
  1. Be respectful. No insults, slurs, targeted harassment, or hate speech. Disagreements are fine — personal attacks are not.
  2. No piracy links or requests. Do not post links to pirated content, torrent files, streaming redirects, or ask for illegal downloads.
  3. No spoilers without warning. Use the spoiler toggle or include a clear spoiler label with episode/chapter details.
  4. Keep it on-topic. Comments should relate to the page’s anime or episode. Off-topic spam, captcha messages, and repetitive one-word posts will be removed.
  5. No doxxing or personal data. Do not post private contact details or attempt to reveal someone’s identity.
  6. No sexual content involving minors. Sexual content is restricted to consenting adults and must follow local law. Anything sexual that involves underage characters or real minors is banned.
  7. No illegal instructions or malicious activity. Do not post instructions for hacking, malware, or other illegal activity.

Examples

Allowed: "This episode had amazing animation — the fight choreography was top tier."
Not allowed: "You're stupid and should kill yourself" — removed for harassment.
Not allowed: Links to external sites that host pirated content.

Spoiler Rules

  • Use a clear warning: "SPOILER: Episode 12 plot details below".
  • On episode/scene pages, assume readers are caught up — hide major reveals for at least 2 weeks after release.

Posting Etiquette

  • Search existing comments before posting to avoid duplicates.
  • Keep language clear; avoid ALL CAPS or excessive emoji.
  • When quoting, attribute the source and keep quotes short.

Moderation & Consequences

  • Moderators may edit or remove comments that break rules.
  • Repeat offenders may receive temporary or permanent bans.
  • In severe cases (threats, doxxing), we may share information with law enforcement.

Last updated: Nov 23, 2025

How to report

If you see a rule-breaking comment, please use the report button next to the comment. Include a short reason — e.g., "Harassment", "Piracy link", or "Spoiler without warning".

Report a comment
If the issue is an immediate threat, contact local authorities. For account or moderation questions: legal@9animeapp.org

Frequently Asked

Q: Can I post download links?
A: No — links to piracy will be removed and the poster may be banned.
Q: How long to hide spoilers?
A: At least 2 weeks for major releases; use judgement for older series.

Formatting & Spoilers

  • Wrap spoilers: [spoiler]...[/spoiler] or use the UI spoiler toggle.
  • Mature topics: add a short trigger/content warning before the comment.
  • Keep single-line comments constructive — avoid multi-post rants.

Contact

For policy questions or appeals: dmca@9animeapp.org
