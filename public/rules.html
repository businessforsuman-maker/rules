Official Community Guidelines

Rules that keep 9Anime respectful, safe, and enjoyable for everyone.

Important: These rules apply to all comments, profiles, and interactions across the platform. Violations may result in warnings, temporary mutes, or permanent bans.

1. Be Respectful & Civil

No personal attacks, harassment, bullying, hate speech, or discrimination of any kind. Respect other viewers, staff, and creators at all times.

2. No Spoilers Without Tags

Major plot points, endings, or character fates must be hidden behind proper spoiler tags. When in doubt — spoiler-tag it.

3. Stay On-Topic

Keep comments relevant to the episode or anime. Off-topic political debates, drama, or unrelated links belong elsewhere.

4. No Illegal or Harmful Content

Prohibited: piracy links, malware, pornography, gore, doxing, threats, or any content violating laws.

5. English Preferred

Primary language is English for moderation. Other languages are welcome in moderation, but excessive non-English flooding may be removed.

6. No Spam or Self-Promotion

Repetitive messages, ads, referral links, or unsolicited promotion will be removed instantly.
