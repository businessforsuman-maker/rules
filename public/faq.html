Frequently Asked Questions

How do I hide spoilers?

Use [spoiler]your text here[/spoiler] or click the spoiler button in the comment box. Spoilers without tags will be deleted.

Why was my comment deleted?

Common reasons: untagged spoilers, toxicity, off-topic discussion, or spam. You’ll receive a notification with the exact reason.

I got banned — can I appeal?

Yes. Permanent bans include an appeal link. Temporary mutes cannot be appealed.

Can I speak languages other than English?

Yes, but English is required for moderation. Repeated non-English flooding may be removed.

Is linking to other streaming sites allowed?

No. Any links to external streaming or download sites are considered spam and removed instantly.

How are the rules enforced?

24/7 automated filters + human moderators. We prioritize fairness and consistency.
